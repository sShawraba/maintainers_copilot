How It Works

The Maintainers Copilot widget is a lightweight, embeddable chatbot that integrates seamlessly with your application. It provides your users with intelligent assistance without requiring them to leave your site.

Integration

To integrate the widget into your site, simply add the loader script:

<!-- Basic integration --> <script src="https://yourdomain.com/widget-loader.js" data-widget-id="demo123"></script>

Or with custom configuration:

<!-- Advanced integration --> <script src="https://yourdomain.com/widget.js"></script> <script> window.MaintainersCopilotWidget.init({ widgetId: 'my-widget', apiUrl: 'https://api.yourdomain.com', position: 'bottom-right', theme: 'light' }); </script>

Features

  • Responsive design (works on all devices)
  • Light and dark theme support
  • Multiple positioning options
  • Message source citations
  • Error handling and recovery
